About PraxisForge

Meet the team of trailblazers who are redefining digital education.

Our Story

PraxisForge is a team of trailblazing technologists, educators, and mentors who have worked with some of the world's most innovative companies—Amazon, Microsoft, and more. As a digital-first and remote-driven collective, we are redefining the way students learn by combining project-based education with real-time mentorship.

Our global outlook and immersive online model ensure that learning happens anytime, anywhere. We believe in breaking down geographical barriers to quality education and creating opportunities for students regardless of their location.

Our mentors and curriculum designers are industry veterans and passionate educators with years of experience in software engineering, data science, AI, product management, and edtech. Every program is infused with their insights, bringing the digital workplace into the digital classroom.

What We Do

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world industry practice. Through live online classes, hands-on remote projects, and ongoing industry mentorship, PraxisForge equips students with future-ready skills.

Whether you're a middle schooler curious about code or a college student prepping for a tech career, we provide guided, outcome-driven online learning experiences that prepare you for the digital future.

We work with educational institutions and directly with students to create customized learning pathways that address specific needs and goals. Our flexible online approach means we can adapt to different time zones, schedules, and learning styles.

How to Pay

1

Open your UPI app

Launch any UPI-enabled app like Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay, BHIM, or your bank's UPI app on your smartphone.

2

Scan QR Code

Use the "Scan & Pay" or "QR Scanner" feature in your app to scan the QR code displayed on the right side.

3

Verify Details

Check that the merchant name shows "RYUZENTECH PRIVATE LIMITED" and the amount is correct before proceeding.

4

Enter PIN & Pay

Enter your UPI PIN to authorize the payment. You'll receive an instant confirmation once the transaction is successful.

5

Save Confirmation

Take a screenshot of the success message and transaction ID for your records. You'll also receive an SMS confirmation.

Security Tip: Never share your UPI PIN with anyone. RyuzenTech will never ask for your PIN or OTP.

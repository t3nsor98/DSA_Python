Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our pricing and payment options.

Is payment refundable?

Yes, we offer a full refund if requested within 7 days before the course begins. Once the program has started, refunds are not available, but you may be eligible to transfer to a future cohort in special circumstances.

Are EMI options available?

Yes, EMI options are available for all 6-month programs. We partner with multiple payment providers to offer flexible installment plans, ranging from 3 to 12 months, subject to eligibility and terms.

Is certification included?

Yes, digital certificates are awarded upon successful completion of your program. These certificates detail the skills acquired and projects completed, and can be shared directly on your LinkedIn profile or other professional platforms.

Do prices include all materials?

Yes, the program fee includes access to all learning materials, recordings of sessions, scheduled mentor hours, project resources, and certification. There are no hidden costs or additional fees for core program components.

Can schools manage group billing?

Yes, custom invoicing is available for school partners. We provide consolidated billing options, volume discounts, and can accommodate specific institutional payment processes. Contact our partnerships team for more information.

Our Mission

At PraxisForge, we are on a mission to transform college students into job-ready professionals through immersive, online, project-driven learning. We go beyond the textbook by integrating academic concepts with hands-on digital experiences that mirror the real-world demands of the tech industry.

Through guided mentorship from leading professionals at top-tier companies like Amazon and Microsoft, students build advanced, portfolio-worthy projects and gain direct insight into modern workflows, tools, and technologies.

Our fully remote programs ensure accessibility, flexibility, and continuity, making it easier for students across geographies to participate, collaborate, and grow into future innovators. Whether you're preparing for your first job or aiming for a competitive edge in your career, PraxisForge helps bridge the gap between campus and corporate—all online.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our college programs.

Are these internships?

No, they're more immersive and practical than traditional internships. Our programs focus on project-based learning with industry mentorship, designed to build both technical skills and professional capabilities. Unlike internships, our programs don't involve working for a specific company, but rather building your own portfolio with guidance from industry experts.

Is there a placement guarantee?

We don't guarantee jobs but do provide career guidance, resume support, and networking opportunities. Our focus is on equipping you with the skills, portfolio, and professional network that significantly enhances your employability. Many of our graduates have secured positions at leading tech companies based on the strength of their portfolio projects and the recommendations of their mentors.

Can colleges co-certify?

Yes, under certain partner agreements. We collaborate with educational institutions to integrate our programs with their curriculum and provide co-certified credentials. This creates additional value for students and enhances the recognition of the skills and experiences gained through our programs.

How are students monitored?

Via digital progress dashboards and mentor feedback reports. We maintain comprehensive tracking of student engagement, project milestones, and skill development. Regular assessments and code reviews ensure that students are making appropriate progress and receiving timely feedback to guide their learning journey.

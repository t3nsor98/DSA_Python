Part I — Information We Collect and Control

RYUZENTECH PVT LTD collects only the information that is necessary to deliver and improve its CRM solutions and customer support. The information is categorized into data that you actively provide, data collected automatically from your interactions, and data obtained from trusted third parties.

Information You Provide to Us

You directly provide information in several ways, including (but not limited to):

  1. Account Signup:
    • Required details: Full name, email address, contact number, company name, country, and password.
    • Optional details: Profile photo, alternate contact details, time zone, and preferred language.
  2. Event Registrations and Form Submissions:
    • Signing up for webinars, demos, CRM training sessions or industry events.
    • Downloading whitepapers, e-books, or other resources.
    • Requesting quotes, product demonstrations, or customer support.
  3. Transactional Information:
    • When you purchase access to our CRM tools or subscribe to our services, we process billing information such as the cardholder's name, billing address, and the last four digits of your credit card (all processed securely via our authorized payment gateway).
  4. Testimonials and Feedback:
    • If you choose to share testimonials or feedback, your name and other identifying information may be displayed publicly (with your prior consent).
  5. Direct Communication:
    • Emails, phone calls, live chats, or support tickets initiated via our websites or apps.
    • Surveys and customer satisfaction questionnaires.

Information Collected Automatically

When you interact with our websites or mobile applications, we automatically log certain data:

  1. Device and Log Information:
    • Browser type, IP address, operating system, device details, time zone, referring URLs, date and time stamps, and pages visited.
    • This usage data helps us analyze trends, administer our website, and improve overall service performance.
  2. Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies:
    • Session Cookies: To maintain session state and provide a seamless user experience.
    • Persistent Cookies: To remember user preferences, language settings, and security authentication tokens.
    • Local Storage and Similar Technologies: To store content preferences or provide functionality when you return to our services.

You can disable cookies via your browser settings; however, this might impair the functionality of some features on our websites.

Information from Third Parties

We may obtain personal data about you from external sources, such as:

  1. Federated Authentication:
    • If you log in using third-party providers like Google, Microsoft, or LinkedIn, those providers may share your basic profile information (name, email address) with us.
  2. Reselling and Referral Partners:
    • If you are introduced to RYUZENTECH PVT LTD via a partner, reseller, or referral program, we may receive your contact details and other relevant data directly from that partner.
  3. Public and Social Media Sources:
    • When you interact with our brand on social media or review sites, the public information (and any content you post) may be collected for analytics or customer support purposes.

Purposes for Using Your Information

Your information is used by RYUZENTECH PVT LTD to:

  • Provide and Maintain Our CRM Services: Create and manage your account, facilitate CRM workflows, and offer tailored features.
  • Customer Communication: Notify you about account changes, product updates, security alerts, and promotional offers related to our CRM.
  • Service Improvements: Analyze usage data to enhance our products, troubleshooting issues, and drive innovation.
  • Personalization: Customize your experience by displaying relevant content, recommendations, or integrated third-party services.
  • Security and Fraud Prevention: Monitor for potentially unauthorized or malicious use of your account and safeguard your data.
  • Legal and Regulatory Compliance: Process data required for legal, accounting, or regulatory obligations.

Legal Bases for Processing Personal Data

Depending on your location (e.g., if you are within the European Economic Area), our processing of personal data is underpinned by one or several of the following legal grounds:

  • Contractual Necessity: Processing that is essential for the delivery of our CRM services.
  • Consent: Where you have explicitly consented to the processing of your personal data (subject to withdrawal at any time).
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing necessary to fulfill our legitimate business interests, provided that such interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Legal Obligations: Processing required to comply with applicable laws or court orders.

Your Choices Regarding Your Information

You have control over the personal information you share with RYUZENTECH PVT LTD:

  • Managing Communication: You may opt out of non-essential marketing emails by using the unsubscribe links provided in each email. Essential transactional emails will still be sent.
  • Cookie Preferences: You may adjust your browser's settings to refuse non-essential cookies. Note that this might limit certain functionalities on our website.
  • Data Accuracy: You can access and update your account information through your user dashboard.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw your permission at any time, though this will not affect processing already carried out.

Retention of Personal Data

RYUZENTECH PVT LTD retains your personal information only for as long as it is needed for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. Specifically:

  • Active Accounts: Your information is kept in active databases as long as you maintain an account.
  • Inactive or Terminated Accounts: Once your account is closed, we begin a routine deletion process:
    • Data is removed from our active systems within six months.
    • Backups including your data will be securely deleted or anonymized after a further three months.

Part II — Information Processed on Your Behalf

When you use our CRM services, you may entrust RYUZENTECH PVT LTD with sensitive information about your business contacts, customers, or clients. This information is defined as "service data."

Service Data: What It Is and How We Process It

  • Scope: Service data includes any data related to your business operations processed through our CRM services (such as customer records, relationship histories, sales leads, and interaction logs).
  • Purpose: We process your service data strictly to perform the services you have requested, such as managing client interactions, tracking sales pipelines, or analyzing customer behavior.
  • Method: Processing is carried out either on our secure servers or through third-party providers under strict confidentiality agreements.

Ownership and Your Control of Service Data

You retain full ownership of all service data you entrust to RYUZENTECH PVT LTD. To ensure you remain in control:

  • You may access, modify, or move your data at any time.
  • You can connect your CRM data to integrated third-party applications.
  • You may request an export or deletion of your service data at any point.

Retention and Deletion of Service Data

  • Active Use: Your service data is stored and maintained as long as you actively use our CRM services.
  • After Termination: Upon termination of your account, service data is purged from active systems during our next scheduled clean-up (typically within six months) and then removed from backups three months thereafter.

Data Subject Rights and Requests

If you are located in jurisdictions that grant you rights in respect of your data (e.g., under the GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct any inaccuracies in your information.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we process your personal data.
  • Portability: Obtain your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Object: Object to our processing of your data for legitimate interests or direct marketing.

To exercise these rights, please contact us as described in the Contact Information section.
